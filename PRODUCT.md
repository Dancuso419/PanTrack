# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Everyday people managing personal finances — students, employees, freelancers, small-business owners — mostly in Nigeria (NGN default) but multi-currency. They open PanTrack to answer "where did my money go, and am I still on budget?" in seconds, often on a phone, between other tasks. Low patience for friction; they are not accountants.

## Product Purpose
Track every penny (income + expenses), set per-category budgets with 80%/100% alerts, and see spending clearly through dashboards, analytics, and reports. Success = a user trusts the numbers, catches overspending early, and comes back weekly without being nagged. A short marketing landing page (register `brand` locally) exists to convert logged-out visitors.

## Brand Personality
Calm, confident, friendly. Three words: **trustworthy, approachable, modern.** It should feel like a well-made consumer fintech app — reassuring, tactile, never intimidating or "enterprise spreadsheet."

## Anti-references
- Dense enterprise accounting tools (QuickBooks, SAP) — intimidating, gray, form-heavy.
- Crypto/trading dashboards — dark, neon, hype-driven, volatile-feeling.
- Generic Bootstrap admin templates — flat, characterless, obviously templated.
- Over-blurred glassmorphism landing pages.

## Design Principles
1. **The number is the hero.** Money figures are the most important thing on any screen; type scale and contrast serve legibility of data first.
2. **Tactile calm.** Soft, physical surfaces (neumorphic) make the app feel gentle and premium — but never at the cost of readability.
3. **Color means something.** Violet = brand/action, green = income/positive, red = expense/over-budget, amber = warning. Never decorative.
4. **Reassure, don't alarm.** Budget warnings inform without panic; empty states teach the next step.
5. **Fast to read, fast to act.** Primary action per screen is always obvious; the tool disappears into the task.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA. Neumorphism's known weakness is low contrast — so body text hits ≥4.5:1 and interactive/data elements carry real color contrast, never shadow-only affordance. Every control has a visible focus ring. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` support. Color is never the sole signal (pair with icon/label/text).
